Saudi Arabia and the United Arab Emirates have taken steps toward joining the Iran war, the Wall Street Journal [reported](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/terminal/TCDMO0PV4WE8), potentially signaling an escalation of the fighting.

Saudi Arabia agreed to give the US military access to King Fahd Air Base, the WSJ reported, citing people familiar with the matter, an apparent reversal after saying its bases couldn’t be used to attack its longtime rival.
